## CRediT Explanation

CRediT means Contributor Roles Taxonomy. It is a standardized author-contribution system with 14 roles, including Conceptualization, Data curation, Formal analysis, Funding acquisition, Investigation, Methodology, Project administration, Resources, Software, Supervision, Validation, Visualization, Writing - original draft, and Writing - review & editing.

Source references:

- NISO CRediT standard page: https://www.niso.org/standards-committees/credit
- CRediT role definitions: https://credit.niso.org/contributor-roles-defined/
