# Author Reply Evidence Folder

Store saved corresponding-author replies here when they are used to update
`metadata/corresponding_author_signoff_tracker.tsv`.

Accepted evidence examples:

- `.eml` saved email replies
- signed `.docx` or `.pdf` confirmations
- plain-text `.txt` or `.md` records copied from an email thread

Do not store passwords, tokens, unrelated private clinical data, or patient
identifiers in this folder.
